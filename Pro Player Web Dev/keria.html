Biography

Ryu "Keria" Min-Seok is a professional League of Legends player, currently playing for T1 (previously SKTelecom T1). Before becoming a professional, he was widely considered the best support player in Korea by many high ranking, aspiring players and professionals alike. At such a young age, his situational awareness and reactive playstyle was unlike anything the community had ever seen. In 2018, Keria started his professional career with DRX (DragonX).
