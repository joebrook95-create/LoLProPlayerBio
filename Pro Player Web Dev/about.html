The Project Behind the Page

About This Project

Welcome to my League of Legends Pro-Player Biography website! This is my first ever web development project, created as part of a college assignment. The goal was to design and build a multi-page website from scratch using HTML and CSS — and being a fan of League of Legends, I thought profiling some of the game's most iconic professional players would make for a topic I was genuinely excited to work on.


I'm proud of how far this project came from its early drafts. The players featured — Baus, Caps, Faker, and Keria — each represent something different about what makes professional League of Legends so compelling to follow, and it was a pleasure to research and write about them. In the future, I will be adding more players, pages and updating information when appropriate. I hope you enjoy exploring the site as much as I enjoyed building it.
