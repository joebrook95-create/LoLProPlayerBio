Biography

Rasmus "Caps" Borregaard Winther is a professional player currently playing for G2 Esports in his 7th year with the organisation. He is often referred to as "Claps" when playing well, and "Craps" when not playing so well. Caps is widely regarded as the best Western League of Legends player of all time.
